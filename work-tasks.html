Copilot Tasks, for Work

Long-running task conversations — not pipelines. Copilot handles the graph of your workstreams, loops back when things change, and brings you in only when it matters.

Task conversations — living threads, not one-shot pipelines
Cross-app reasoning — Teams resolved an unanswered email
Provenance — every claim traces to a file, cell, or thread
One checkpoint — approve before sending externally
Loop-backs — stakeholders reply, the conversation continues
Change detection — flags when data shifted since last meeting
Ambient — "I'll watch and bring it to you when it matters"
Home + Tasks
Tasks live as a separate concept from Conversations. Agents, Tasks, Chats — three distinct things in one rail.
Task Conversation
Each task is a long-running conversation, not a one-shot pipeline. Copilot chats with you about what it found, what it did, and what's next.
Cross-App Intelligence
Copilot found that Teams resolved an unanswered email. It matched the VP's question to the latest data. This reasoning spans apps.
Provenance + One Checkpoint
Every claim cited. The only approval is sending to a VP. Everything else was handled.
What's Next
After sending, Copilot tells you what it'll watch for and preps you for related meetings. The conversation keeps running.
Loop-Back
Sarah replied. Copilot drafted a response and brought it back into the same conversation. Work isn't linear — the system keeps up.
