Platform Investment Proposal — H2 FY26

Engineering Platform Team · Prepared for CVP Review · Draft v1.0

1. Executive Summary

This proposal recommends a $12M investment in platform infrastructure for H2 FY26, focused on three pillars: developer velocity, AI workload scaling, and reliability engineering. The investment addresses a growing gap between product ambition and platform capacity that has resulted in 23% longer deployment cycles and 3 P0 incidents in the last quarter.

2. Problem Statement

The current platform stack was architected for 40 services. We now run 127 services across 3 regions.

  • Deployment time: 4.2 hours, up from 1.8 hours
  • Incident rate: 3 P0 incidents in Q1
  • Developer NPS: Dropped from 72 to 41

3. Proposed Investment

Developer Velocity ($4.5M): Unified CI/CD with canary deployments, automated rollback. Target: under 45 minutes.

AI Workload Scaling ($5M): Dedicated GPU tier with auto-scaling. Current AI workloads consume 38% of compute.

Reliability Engineering ($2.5M): Observability, chaos engineering, SRE expansion 4→8. Target: P0s <1/quarter.

4. ROI & Payback

Based on current incident costs ($180K per P0) and developer time savings, projected payback period is 14 months.

5. Ask

We are requesting $12M in H2 FY26 budget allocation with quarterly milestone reviews.
