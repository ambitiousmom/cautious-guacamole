# Vegetarian, No Eggs, Dairy OK

## Dietary Rules
- Strictly vegetarian — no meat, no fish, no eggs
- Dairy is OK (paneer, yogurt, cream, ghee, cheese, butter)
- No specific allergies

## Favorite Cuisines (in order)
1. South Indian
2. North Indian
3. Mexican / Chipotle-style
4. American (breakfast-for-dinner)
5. Italian
6. Asian

## Household
- 4 people
- Kids like: dosa, pancakes, quesadillas, mac and cheese, paneer butter masala
- Adults like: sambar, rasam, chole, dal tadka, pad thai (veg)

## Weekly Nutrition Goals
- Protein: ~400g/week (~57g/day) — HIGH PRIORITY
- Fiber: ~175g/week (~25g/day) — HIGH PRIORITY
- Calories: ~14,000 kcal/week (~2,000/day)
